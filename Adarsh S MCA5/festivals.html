Festivals in india

India celebrates a multitude of religious, seasonal, and national festivals, with key examples including Diwali (Festival of Lights), Holi (Festival of Colors), Eid, Ganesh Chaturthi, ** Durga Puja**, Dussehra, and the national holidays of Republic Day, Independence Day, and Gandhi Jayanti. These festivals often mark significant religious events, harvest seasons, or patriotic milestones, each with unique traditions and celebrations
